Strawberry Pretzel Salad

Description

Dessert!!!! The best word in the dictionary and the most important(obviously).

Straberries and Pretzels the key ingredients in this simple but awesomely-tastic desert as well as butter cream cheese and of course some suger, because what's a dessert without that sweet sweet tooth. The combination of these ingredients will surely wake up your tounge with contrasting textures and flavors, that you'd think wouldn't go together, just kidding c'mon strawberries and pretzels are basically peanut butter and jelly.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups crushed pretzels
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry flavored Jell-O
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen strawberries

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Stir crushed pretzels, melted butter, and 3 tablespoons sugar together until well-combined; mix well and press mixture into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 8 to 10 minutes; set aside to cool.
  4. Place cream cheese and 1 cup sugar in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until smooth; fold in whipped topping. Spread mixture onto cooled crust.
  5. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in still-frozen strawberries and allow to set briefly. Pour and spread over cream cheese layer; refrigerate until set, at least 2 hours.
